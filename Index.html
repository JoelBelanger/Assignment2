Scottie Barnes

Biography

Scottie Barnes, born August 1st 2001 in West Palm Beach (Florida), is an African American basketball rookie who has made a name for himself over the past few years, with his first big achievement winning the gold medal on the United States team at FIBA 2017, along with winning gold at FIBA 2018 and 2019. After this he played on the Montverde Highschool basketball team (2019-2020), which gave him a lot of recognition that led to him being drafted by the Toronto Raptors in 2021. He was picked 4th overall, and is performing very good in the NBA to this day
